Fertility Rate: Global Perspectives and Malaysia’s Journey

How Does Fertility Vary Across the World in 2023?

Darker shades indicate higher fertility rates, revealing significant regional differences. Countries in Africa exhibit much higher fertility rates compared to developed nations like the United States, Australia, and China. The map allows us to see global trends and identify regions where population growth is most significant.

Does Economic Growth Affect Fertility Rates?

The size of the circles represents population size, while the color indicates the continent. Countries with higher GDPs such as Singapore and Australia tend to have lower fertility rates, whereas countries like Yemen with lower GDP have higher fertility rates.

How Does Fertility Rate Vary by Age in Malaysia?

The most significant decline can be seen in the 20-24 age group, followed closely by the 15-19 and 25-29 groups. These trends reflect Malaysia's transition towards lower fertility rates and smaller family sizes, typical of developing economies.
